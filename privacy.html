Privacy Policy

TapScore — last updated 28 March 2026

TapScore is a badminton score counter app for Android and iOS, developed by Klemen Dobrota. This policy explains what data the app does and does not collect.

Data we collect

None. TapScore does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. There are no user accounts, no analytics, no advertising SDKs, and no backend server.

Device permissions used

  • Keep screen awake — prevents the screen from turning off during a match. No data is read or transmitted.
  • Haptic feedback — vibrates the device on each tap. No data is read or transmitted.

SmashRating integration (optional)

At the end of a match you may choose to log the result to SmashRating. Tapping this option opens the SmashRating website in your browser with the match score pre-filled in the URL. TapScore does not send any data directly — your browser handles the request. SmashRating's own privacy policy applies once you leave the app.

Third-party services

TapScore does not integrate any third-party analytics, crash reporting, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's privacy

The app does not collect any data from any user, including children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published at this URL with a revised date at the top.
